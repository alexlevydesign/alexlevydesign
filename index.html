Hi, I'm Alex Levy

I am a designer and student at Syracuse University exploring the convergence of design and technology with people and business

Download Resume

I am an explorer.
Whenever I am in a new place — whether it's a small hotel or a large college campus — I love exploring every nook and cranny of that place. My fascination with exploration extends to my design process, too. Whether I'm designing part of a user interface that won a hackathon section or creating infographics for an advertising campaign, I explore many different solutions. I guide my exploration by putting myself in the user or audience's shoes to better understand the problems that need to be solved. When I'm designing, I ask myself what problems the user has and why. These questions drive my design process because the user is the foundation of my design decisions.
