Personal Brand

Roles

Designer • Content writer

Tools

Adobe InDesign • Adobe Photoshop • Adobe Illustrator • Blender

Outcome

I designed my personal brand to brand myself as a user-centered designer. I designed everything, from my new logo and patterns to stationery and social media posts, with this brand goal.

View final design

The Situation

I designed a personal brand system and applied it across various formats and mediums, such as social posts, stationery, a 3D printed branded item, and a new logo. The key parts of myself that I wanted to capture in this brand were my focus on user-centered design and my friendly personality.

Background

I had previously designed bits and pieces of my personal brand, but I threw most of that out the window and designed a completely new brand.

The design process

Logos, wordmarks, and lockups

My logo and lockup went through a lot of iteration before I arrived at a final design, which merges elements from past iterations.

Brand system

Design decisions

  • I took the negative space from my logo and made it into a pattern and a motif that was both decorative and functional, such as in my greeting card.
  • I made my social posts very straightforward and took out distracting elements to make it more likely that people will want to read them.
