About Me

I am a storyteller.
I am an explorer.
I am a designer.

Whether I'm working with teammates to design a user interface that won a hackathon section or creating infographics for an advertising campaign, I don't design blindly. I consider all the elements involved, such as the audience, the client, and the context (just to name a few). This requires exploration, which is guided by putting myself in the user or audience's shoes from the perspective of the client to better understand the problems that need to be solved. Understanding these problems is the foundation of my design process.
